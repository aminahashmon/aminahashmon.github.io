Who is Aminah?

I'm an 18 year old woman from Detroit, Michigan. An accounting major at Western Michigan University, looking forward to life post graduation. I plan on eventually starting a family, having a rewarding career in corporate America, and starting my own Accounting Firm. Triple A-Minah is a blog all about my thoughts on everything- from politics to fashion. My blog is for people of all interests and backgrounds. I tackle political and social issues from different viewpoints and pose the questions that most people don't want to ask. As a young, African-American woman from the inner city, I think that's its important to show the world how informed, and inquisitive young people can be. Thanks for logging on!
