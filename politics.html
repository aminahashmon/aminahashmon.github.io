Big Parties- Big Problems: Breaking Away from the Democrats and Republicans

  • As someone who neither identifies as a Democrat or Republican, I felt it necessary to search for presidential candidiates who were not from the two largest political parties.
  • The recent tom-foolery brought by Trump and Clinton's childish antics has only driven me further from the two parties.
  • Recently, I discovered Jill Stein- a member of the Green Party.
  • Gary Johnson- a member of the Libertarian Party- is shaking up the polls with an 8 percent approval rating from voters.
  • Currently, only 42 state representatives identify as third-party candidates.
  • I think its important that voters know they have other options besides Trump and Clinton.
  • There are over twenty different third-party political groups.
